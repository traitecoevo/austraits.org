By providing a curated, open source and FAIR big data resource, AusTraits aims to transform research on the Australian flora. This page tracks our impact to date. The page was last updated on 2023-03-01. For the latest impact, see links provided below.

Data Descriptor

The original paper describing AusTraits data was published in Scientific Data:

  • Falster, Gallagher, Wenk, Wright, Indiarto, Andrew, … (2021) AusTraits, a curated plant trait database for the Australian flora. Scientific data: 8 (1), 1-20 10.1038/s41597-021-01006-6.
